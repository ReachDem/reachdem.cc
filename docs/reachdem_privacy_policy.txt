By accessing or using the Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

1. Who We Are

ReachDem is a communication and engagement platform designed to help businesses, teams, entrepreneurs, and organizations manage digital communication, customer engagement, contact data, audience segmentation, campaign workflows, analytics, public profile pages, and related interactions across supported channels.

Depending on the Services used, ReachDem may provide functionality relating to contact and audience management, group and segment organization, messaging campaigns, link shortening and tracking, analytics and reporting, public profile and link pages, digital business card experiences, integrations, API access, workflow automation, and AI-assisted features.

For the purposes of applicable privacy laws, ReachDem may act either as a data controller for personal data collected for its own business operations, or as a data processor or service provider when processing personal data on behalf of its customers.

2. Scope of This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to personal data collected through our website, account registration and account administration, customer onboarding, dashboard usage, public profile pages, communication tools, link and campaign analytics, contact management features, support interactions, integrations, APIs, and other related business operations.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party websites, services, or platforms that may be linked to, integrated with, or accessible through our Services. Those third parties operate under their own terms and privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them separately.

3. Personal Data We Collect

We may collect personal data directly from you, automatically when you use the Services, from our customers, from third-party integrations, and from service providers that support our business operations.

The personal data we collect may include your name, email address, phone number, company name, job title, business contact details, account credentials, profile details, workspace information, billing information, subscription information, support inquiries, survey responses, and any other information you choose to provide to us.

Where customers use ReachDem to import, create, organize, or manage contact databases, we may process contact information such as names, phone numbers, email addresses, business details, addresses, birthdays, tags, segmentation attributes, custom fields, campaign preferences, communication history, and other metadata associated with a contact record.

When the Services are used to create, send, route, or analyze communications, we may process personal data relating to the sender and recipient, including names, addresses, channel identifiers, message content, subject lines, attached media, delivery timestamps, delivery status, engagement events, unsubscribe events, and related campaign metadata.

When users interact with ReachDem pages, shortened links, digital business card pages, or Links by ReachDem profiles, we may collect data such as page views, link clicks, referral sources, approximate geolocation inferred from IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, session activity, timestamps, campaign attribution parameters, and other interaction metrics.

We also collect technical and usage information when users access the Services, including IP address, log data, browser settings, device identifiers, access times, navigation patterns, API logs, crash reports, security signals, and diagnostic information necessary to maintain, secure, and improve the Services.

If payments are processed in connection with the Services, we may receive billing-related information, payment status, invoicing details, subscription records, and limited transaction metadata. Full payment card details are typically processed by third-party payment providers and are not stored by us unless expressly stated otherwise.

Where AI-enabled features are available, we may process user prompts, instructions, campaign context, contact-related context, analytics inputs, generated outputs, user feedback, and related metadata necessary to provide those features.
